Web 3.0

Web3.0 is the next-generation internet coined by Polkadot founder of Ethereum. Web3 will change the whole pattern of the internet. Web3 enhances the internet

Some elements of web3
• Native built-in payments
•Self governing
•Verifiable
•Stateful
•Permissionless
•Anonymous single sign in

Web3 applications run on blockchains, decentralized networks of many peers to peer nodes or a hybrid of the two that creates decentralized apps which are frequently used in the web3 domain to describe these.

On Web3 the blockchain is where your data is stored, when you decide to leave a platform you can take your prestige with you and plug it into a different interface that better reflects your ideals.

Web3 is just the beginning of our efforts to build better the web, as we continue to enhance the underlying structure, the web's future looks promising.
